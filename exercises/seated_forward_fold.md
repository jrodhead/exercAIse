# Seated Forward Fold

## How to Perform
1. Sit with legs extended, feet flexed.
2. Hinge at hips, reach toward feet, keeping back long.
3. Hold at a comfortable stretch.

## Cues
- Relax neck and shoulders.
- Breathe deeply.
- Do not force the stretch.
