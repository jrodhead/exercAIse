# Hollow Hold

## How to Perform
1. Lie on your back, arms extended overhead and legs extended.
2. Press your lower back into the floor.
3. Lift your shoulders and legs slightly off the mat, keeping your core tight.
4. Hold for the prescribed time, breathing steadily.
5. Modify by bending your knees if needed.

## Variations
- Weighted Hollow Hold (hold a light dumbbell or ruck)
- Alternating arm/leg hollow hold
- Hollow rock

## Tips
- Keep your lower back pressed into the floor.
- Focus on quality over duration.
- Relax your neck and shoulders.
