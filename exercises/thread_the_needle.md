# Thread the Needle Stretch

## How to Perform
1. Start on hands and knees.
2. Slide right arm under left, palm up, shoulder and ear to floor.
3. Hold, then switch sides.

## Cues
- Keep hips high and square.
- Relax into the stretch.
- Breathe deeply.
